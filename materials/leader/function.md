# Use of Generative AI

## 1. Answering Questions
Generative AI can answer various types of questions, from general queries to more specific ones. Below are some prompts you can use to discuss strategic planning:

| No. | Topic                            | Prompt |
|-----|----------------------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| 1   | Goals and Vision                 | Explain UTM's long-term goals and how strategic planning can help achieve this vision.                                 |
| 2   | SWOT Analysis                    | Describe how a SWOT analysis (Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats) can assist UTM in formulating strategies.                                    |
| 3   | SMART Objectives                 | State the SMART objectives (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound) to be achieved through this strategic planning.                 |
| 4   | Stakeholder Engagement           | Describe how UTM can engage stakeholders in the strategic planning process.                                                            |
| 5   | Monitoring and Evaluation        | Explain the monitoring and evaluation mechanisms to measure the success of UTM's strategic plan.                                                 |
| 6   | Change and Adaptation            | Explain how UTM will adapt to changes in the industry or macro environment.                                          |
| 7   | Innovation and Growth            | State innovative initiatives or projects UTM can include in the strategic planning to drive growth.                                    |
| 8   | Risk and Crisis Management       | Describe the main risks that might be faced and explain how UTM plans to manage crises. |
| 9   | Resources                        | Explain how UTM will use available resources to achieve strategic objectives. |
| 10  | Collaboration and Partnerships   | State the collaboration or partnership opportunities that can be explored to strengthen UTM's strategic plan. |

## 2. Creative Content
Creative content refers to the creation of original and innovative material, which can involve various forms such as text, graphics, audio, and video. It often involves new ideas, unconventional approaches, and the use of creativity to solve problems or convey messages in unique and engaging ways. Here are types of creative content that can be produced:

## 3. Summarizing Information
Generative AI can help summarize articles, books, or other documents. Here are some example instructions for summarizing information from various sources:

| No. | Topic               | Prompt |
|-----|---------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| 1   | Academic Article    | Summarize this article discussing technological innovations at UTM and how they affect the quality of education and research.         |
| 2   | Research Report     | Summarize this report evaluating the effectiveness of UTM's industrial training programs in enhancing graduate employability.                |
| 3   | Book                | Summarize this chapter from a book on the history of higher education in Malaysia, specifically discussing UTM's role in providing educational access to Bumiputera. |
| 4   | News Article        | Summarize this article reporting UTM students' success in an international competition and the impact of this success on the university's reputation.           |
| 5   | Annual Report       | Summarize this section from UTM's annual report covering academic achievements, research, and community services from the past year.                 |
| 6   | Interview           | Summarize this interview with a senior UTM lecturer discussing curriculum changes and teaching approaches in the digital era.                |
| 7   | Case Study          | Summarize this case study examining UTM's student mobility program and its impact on students' soft skills development.                                |
| 8   | Policy Document     | Summarize this policy document outlining UTM's strategies and initiatives to improve teaching and learning quality.                            |
